<p><p>Mobile payment technologies can be categorized into two main types: proximity payments and remote payments. Proximity payments refer to transactions that occur in close physical proximity between the customer and the merchant, often using near-field communication (NFC) or QR codes. This allows customers to make payments by simply tapping their phones or scanning codes. On the other hand, remote payments enable transactions to be conducted from a distance, such as online purchases or bill payments through mobile apps. These technologies provide convenient and secure ways for customers to make payments using their mobile devices.</p></p>
