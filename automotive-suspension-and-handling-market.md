<p><p>Automotive Suspension and Handling market types include Control Arms, Solid/Live Axle, Multilink, and others. Control Arms use a hinged connection to the chassis and wheel hub to allow for vertical movement. Solid/Live Axle systems use a single beam to connect wheels on the same axle. Multilink systems use multiple arms and links to improve handling and comfort. Other types include torsion beam and semi-trailing arm suspensions, each offering unique advantages in terms of stability, handling, and comfort.</p></p>
