<p><p>Auto ventilated seats refer to seats in vehicles that have built-in fans to help circulate air and keep the occupant cool and comfortable. There are two main types of auto ventilated seats based on the fan design: radial fans seats and axial fans seats. Radial fans seats utilize fans that blow air outwards in a circular motion, while axial fans seats use fans that blow air parallel to the fan's axis. Both types of ventilated seats provide a more comfortable driving experience by reducing sweat and maintaining a comfortable temperature.</p></p>
