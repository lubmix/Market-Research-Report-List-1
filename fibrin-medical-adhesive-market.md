<p><p>The Fibrin Medical Adhesive Market can be categorized into four types based on the formulation: Water-based, Solvent-based, Solids, and Hot Melt-based. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and easy to clean up, while solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities. Solid adhesives come in the form of sticks or pellets and are typically heated to be applied, while hot melt-based adhesives are melted before application and solidify upon cooling. Each type of adhesive offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in the medical field.</p></p>
