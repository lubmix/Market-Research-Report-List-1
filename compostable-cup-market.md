<p><p>The compostable cup market includes two main types of products: paper-coated and thermoforming cups. Paper-coated cups are made from a combination of paper and a thin coating to provide moisture resistance. They are often used for cold beverages. Thermoforming cups, on the other hand, are made from a biodegradable plastic material that can withstand high temperatures, making them suitable for hot beverages. Both types of cups are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastic cups and are gaining popularity in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
