<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A single-stage torque converter is a type of torque converter that uses only one set of stator and turbine blades to transfer power from the engine to the transmission. This design is ideal for applications where space is limited and a simpler design is preferred. On the other hand, a multistage torque converter utilizes multiple sets of stator and turbine blades to provide a greater range of torque multiplication and efficiency. This type of torque converter is typically used in heavy-duty industrial and engineering vehicles where high torque requirements are needed.</p></p>
