<p><p>Organoclay powder is categorized into three types based on polarity: low polarity (Type I), medium polarity (Type II), and high polarity (Type III). Low polarity organoclay powders are used for non-polar to slightly polar systems, while medium polarity powders are suitable for medium polar systems. High polarity organoclay powders are designed for highly polar systems. Each type of organoclay powder is tailored to provide optimal performance in specific applications based on the polarity of the system being treated.</p></p>
