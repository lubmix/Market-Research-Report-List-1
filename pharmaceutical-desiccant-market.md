<p><p>Pharmaceutical desiccants play a crucial role in protecting pharmaceutical products from moisture degradation. The main types of desiccants used in the pharmaceutical industry include silica gel, activated carbon, clay desiccant, and molecular sieves. Silica gel is a highly porous form of silica that effectively absorbs moisture. Activated carbon is known for its high adsorption capacity. Clay desiccant is a natural mineral that can absorb moisture effectively. Molecular sieves are synthetic materials with uniform pores that can selectively adsorb water molecules.</p></p>
