<p><p>Water sports safety helmets come in two main types: open-face helmets and full-face helmets. Open-face helmets provide protection to the top and sides of the head, while leaving the face exposed. They are popular among water sports enthusiasts for their lightweight and breathable design. On the other hand, full-face helmets provide maximum protection by covering the entire head and face. They are commonly used in more extreme water sports activities where the risk of injury is higher. Both types of helmets play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of individuals participating in water sports.</p></p>
