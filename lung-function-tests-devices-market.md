<p><p>The lung function tests devices market includes various types of devices used to assess pulmonary health. Spirometers are commonly used to measure lung capacity and airflow. Breath CO monitors measure carbon monoxide levels in exhaled breath, indicating smoking or exposure to pollutants. Oscillometers assess lung function by measuring airway resistance and elasticity. Ergospirometers combine lung function testing with exercise assessment. Dose controlled drug nebulizers deliver medication directly to the lungs. Other devices include peak flow meters and electronic stethoscopes. These devices play a vital role in diagnosing and monitoring respiratory conditions.</p></p>
