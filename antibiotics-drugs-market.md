<p><p>The Antibiotics Drugs Market comprises several types of drugs. Phenicols, such as chloramphenicol, work by inhibiting protein synthesis. Quinolones, including ciprofloxacin, hinder the replication of bacterial DNA. Macrolides, like erythromycin, impede protein synthesis. Sulfonamides, such as sulfamethoxazole, inhibit folic acid synthesis. Aminoglycosides like gentamicin interfere with bacteria's protein synthesis. Tetracyclines, including doxycycline, block protein synthesis. Beta-lactam antibiotics, which include penicillin, disrupt cell wall synthesis. Lastly, other antibiotics encompass various drugs with alternative mechanisms, such as vancomycin. These diverse types of antibiotics offer different ways to target and combat bacterial infections.</p></p>
