<p><strong>The Rubber Abrasion Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotating</li><li>Non-Rotating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber abrasion testers can be categorized into two main types: rotating and non-rotating. Rotating testers involve the use of a rotating drum or wheel to simulate the wear and tear that occurs on rubber materials in real-life situations. Non-rotating testers, on the other hand, use a stationary surface against which the rubber material is rubbed or pressed to evaluate its resistance to abrasion. Both types of testers play a crucial role in assessing the durability and performance of rubber materials.</p></p>
