<p><p>Kraft Sealing Tape comes in two main market types: Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape and Water Free Kraft Paper Tape. Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape requires moisture to activate the adhesive and create a secure seal, making it ideal for heavy-duty packaging. On the other hand, Water Free Kraft Paper Tape has a pressure-sensitive adhesive that does not require water for activation, allowing for a quicker and more convenient sealing process. Both types of tape offer strong adhesion and are commonly used for shipping and packaging applications.</p></p>
