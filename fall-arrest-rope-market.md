<p><p>Fall arrest ropes are categorized into three main types: static, dynamic, and semi-static. Static ropes are designed to provide consistent resistance and are typically used for activities such as rappelling. Dynamic ropes have some stretch to absorb the shock of a fall, making them ideal for climbing and mountaineering. Semi-static ropes offer a balance between the two, providing some stretch while still maintaining a level of consistency. Each type is used for specific activities based on their unique characteristics and demands.</p></p>
