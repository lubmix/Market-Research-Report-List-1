<p><strong>The Protective Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adhesive-coated</li><li>Self-adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protective films can be classified into two main types: Adhesive-coated and self-adhesive films. Adhesive-coated films require an additional layer of adhesive to be applied during installation, providing a strong bond to the surface. On the other hand, self-adhesive films come pre-coated with adhesive, making them easier to apply without the need for extra adhesive. Both types of films provide protection against scratches, dirt, and other damage to surfaces, making them popular choices in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
