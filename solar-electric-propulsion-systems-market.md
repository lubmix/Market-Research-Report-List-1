<p><p>Solar Electric Propulsion Systems Market Types include Hall Effect Thruster (HET), Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT), and Others. HETs use electric fields to accelerate ions and produce thrust. PPTs generate thrust by ionizing propellant gas and then expelling it through a magnetic nozzle. Other types of propulsion systems may include Gridded Ion Thrusters, Field Emission Electric Propulsion, and Magnetoplasmadynamic Thrusters. These different types of propulsion systems offer varying levels of efficiency, thrust, and technical capabilities for space missions.</p></p>
