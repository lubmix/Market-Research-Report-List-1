<p><p>IT-enabled healthcare refers to the application of information technology in the healthcare industry, which enhances the quality and efficiency of healthcare delivery. First and foremost, the increasing adoption of EHRs and digital health solutions by healthcare providers is driving market growth. These solutions not only streamline administrative tasks but also improve patient care by providing a comprehensive view of the patient's medical history.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising demand for personalized medicine and preventive healthcare is fueling the implementation of IT-enabled healthcare solutions. These solutions enable healthcare providers to collect and analyze large amounts of patient data, facilitating the development of personalized treatment plans and proactive healthcare interventions.</p><p>Additionally, the growing geriatric population and the prevalence of chronic diseases are also contributing to market growth. IT-enabled healthcare solutions assist in remote patient monitoring and telehealth services, allowing elderly and chronically ill patients to receive quality care at home.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing an increased integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) in healthcare IT solutions. AI-powered chatbots, predictive analytics, and image recognition systems are revolutionizing medical diagnostics and improving the efficiency of healthcare operations.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the adoption of telehealth and virtual care solutions. The need for social distancing and remote healthcare consultations has led to a surge in demand for telemedicine services, boosting the growth of the IT-enabled healthcare market.</p><p>Overall, the IT-enabled healthcare market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period.
