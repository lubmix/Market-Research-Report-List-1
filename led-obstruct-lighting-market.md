<p><p>The LED obstruct lighting market can be categorized into three types: Low Intensity LED Obstruct Light, Medium Intensity LED Obstruct Light, and High Intensity LED Obstruct Light. Low intensity lights are typically used for buildings or structures under 200 feet and emit a steady red light. Medium intensity lights are used for structures between 200 and 500 feet and emit a flashing white light. High intensity lights are used for structures over 500 feet and emit a synchronized flashing white light. These lights are important for aviation safety and ensuring visibility during low-light or poor weather conditions.</p></p>
