<p><p>The automotive hybrid transmission system market can be categorized into two main types: single stage and multi stage. A single stage hybrid transmission system typically consists of only one electric motor and is simpler in design compared to a multi stage system. On the other hand, a multi stage hybrid transmission system incorporates multiple electric motors, providing more flexibility and efficiency in power distribution. Both types of systems aim to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in vehicles through a combination of internal combustion engine and electric motor propulsion.</p></p>
