<p><strong>The Dental Barrier Membrane Bone Graft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Membrane</li><li>Non-absorbable Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental barrier membrane bone graft market consists of two main types: absorbable membrane and non-absorbable membrane. Absorbable membranes are designed to break down over time, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove them. Non-absorbable membranes, on the other hand, are made of materials that do not break down and may need to be removed in a separate procedure. Both types play a crucial role in guided bone regeneration and are used to protect the bone graft material and facilitate new bone formation.</p></p>
