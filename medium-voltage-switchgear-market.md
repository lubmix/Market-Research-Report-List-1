<p><p>The Medium Voltage Switchgear Market includes two main types: Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS) and Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS). AIS are open-air systems that, although relatively inexpensive, require more space and maintenance. On the other hand, GIS are compact and fully enclosed systems, where components are insulated by gas. GIS are more expensive but offer advantages like higher reliability, lower maintenance, and space efficiency. Both types are used to control, protect, and isolate electrical equipment in medium voltage power distribution networks.</p></p>
