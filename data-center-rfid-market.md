<p><p>The Data Center RFID market can be divided into different types of hardware and software. Tags are small devices attached to equipment or assets that transmit information via radio waves. Readers capture the data from tags and provide it to the software. Antennas help to improve the range and accuracy of tag detection. Other hardware includes cabinets, racks, cables, and connectors. Software enables the management, tracking, and analysis of collected data. Altogether, these components play a crucial role in optimizing data center operations and improving efficiency.</p></p>
