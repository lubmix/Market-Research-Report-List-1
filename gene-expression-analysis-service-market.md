<p><p>Gene expression analysis service is a type of service that provides comprehensive information on the expression of genes within various cells, tissues, or organisms. The analysis can be performed using various techniques such as microarray analysis, Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), polymerase chain reaction (PCR), and others.</p><p>The gene expression analysis service market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for personalized medicine and the rising incidence of genetic disorders. The advancements in technology, such as the use of NGS platforms for gene expression analysis, have further propelled market growth. NGS offers advantages such as high-throughput analysis, cost-effectiveness, and accurate results, thereby driving its adoption in gene expression analysis services.</p><p>Moreover, the growing number of research activities in the field of genomics and transcriptomics, along with the increasing collaborations between academic research institutes and biotechnology companies, has also boosted the demand for gene expression analysis services. Furthermore, the rising focus on drug discovery and development is expected to fuel market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a significant shift towards the use of single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) for gene expression analysis. This technique allows the analysis of gene expression in individual cells, providing valuable insights into cellular heterogeneity and function. Additionally, there is a growing trend towards the integration of gene expression analysis with other omics data, such as genomics and proteomics, to gain a comprehensive understanding of biological systems.</p><p>Overall, the gene expression analysis service market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
