<p><p>Gas welding is a type of welding process that uses a fuel gas and oxygen to generate a flame for melting and joining metals. There are three main types of gas welding markets. </p><p>1. Air Acetylene Welding: This process uses a mixture of air and acetylene gas to produce a flame. It is commonly used for small-scale welding and soldering applications.</p><p>2. Oxy Hydrogen Welding: In this process, oxyhydrogen gas (a mixture of oxygen and hydrogen) is used to create a high-temperature flame. It is mainly used for specialized applications like jewelry making and laboratory work.</p><p>3. Oxy Acetylene Welding: This is the most widely used gas welding process. It uses a mixture of oxygen and acetylene gas to create a high-temperature flame for welding and cutting various metals. It is employed in a wide range of industries, including automotive, construction, and metal fabrication.</p></p>
