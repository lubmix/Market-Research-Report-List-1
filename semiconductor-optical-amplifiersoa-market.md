<p><p>Semiconductor Optical Amplifiers (SOA) are used to amplify optical signals in fiber optic communication systems. There are two main types of SOA markets: Erbium Doped Fiber Amplifier (EDFA) and Linear Optical Amplifier. EDFA uses erbium-doped fiber to amplify the signal and is commonly used in long-haul communication systems. Linear optical amplifiers provide a constant gain across a wide range of input signal levels and are used in applications where signal fidelity is critical, such as in metro and access networks.</p></p>
