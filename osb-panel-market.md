<p><p>OSB Panel Market includes four main types: OSB/1, OSB/2, OSB/3, and OSB/4. OSB/1 is used for interior applications where moisture resistance is not a concern. OSB/2 is suitable for structural applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 is designed for structural use in humid conditions and can be used for flooring and roofing. OSB/4 is the most durable and can withstand high levels of humidity and heavy loads, making it ideal for exterior applications such as wall sheathing and subflooring. Each type offers different levels of strength and moisture resistance for various construction needs.</p></p>
