<p><p>Epoxy Resin Carbon Fiber Prepreg market can be categorized into two types based on the type of matrix material used: Thermoset and Thermo-plastic. Thermoset prepregs have a high resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for applications that require high strength and durability. Thermo-plastic prepregs, on the other hand, offer easier processing as they can be reformed and reshaped multiple times. Both types of prepregs cater to different market needs and have their own set of advantages and applications.</p></p>
