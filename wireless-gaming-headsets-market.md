<p><p>Wireless gaming headsets are available in different market types based on the technology used for transmission. Infrared market uses infrared light to transmit audio signals, suitable for short-range connections. M Wave market utilizes microwave technology for medium-range transmission, offering good audio quality and stability. Decimeter Wave market uses higher frequency waves for longer-range connections, ensuring minimal interference and high-quality sound. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of gamers in terms of range, audio quality, and stability.</p></p>
