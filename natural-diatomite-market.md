<p><p>Natural Diatomite can be classified into two main types - Anhydrous Substance and Flux Calcined. Anhydrous Substance refers to diatomite that has not undergone any heating treatments or modifications, retaining its natural properties. On the other hand, Flux Calcined diatomite is processed at high temperatures to improve its properties such as porosity and absorbency. Both types cater to different industrial applications, with Anhydrous Substance being preferred for filtration and Flux Calcined diatomite being used in industries requiring enhanced absorbent properties.</p></p>
