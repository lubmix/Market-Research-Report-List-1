<p><p>Automatic curved sliding doors market includes two main types - single sliding doors and bi-parting doors. Single sliding doors consist of a single panel that slides to the side to open, while bi-parting doors consist of two panels that slide away from each other to create an opening. Single sliding doors are popular in smaller spaces, while bi-parting doors are commonly used in larger entrances where a wider opening is necessary. Both types offer convenience and modern aesthetics in various commercial and public buildings.</p></p>
