<p><p>Ammonium Paratungstate (APT) market can be categorized into three types based on production methods: Neutralization, Tungsten Acid Method, and Thermal Decomposition Method. The Neutralization method involves reacting ammonium metatungstate with ammonium hydroxide. The Tungsten Acid Method involves reacting tungstic acid with ammonium hydroxide. The Thermal Decomposition Method involves thermal decomposition of ammonium metatungstate. Each method produces APT with slightly different properties, leading to different applications in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
