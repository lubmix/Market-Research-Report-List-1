<p><p>Aqueous Film-Forming Foam Concentrate (AFFF) is available in different types based on their fluoroprotein concentration levels - 1%, 3%, and 6%. These concentrations determine the foam's effectiveness in suppressing fires of different types and intensities. The 1% fluoroprotein AFFF is suitable for combating ordinary combustible fires, while the 3% fluoroprotein AFFF is suitable for combating polar solvent fires and hydrocarbon fuel fires. The 6% fluoroprotein AFFF is designed for use in combating gasoline, diesel, and other hydrocarbon fuel fires.</p></p>
