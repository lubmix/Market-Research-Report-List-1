<p><strong>The Digital Print Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital print films are classified into two main categories: solvent-based films and water-based films. Solvent-based films are designed for outdoor applications and are known for their durability and weather resistance. They are ideal for use on surfaces such as vehicles, walls, and windows. On the other hand, water-based films are more environmentally friendly and are commonly used for indoor applications like posters, decals, and banners. They offer excellent color reproduction and are easy to handle and install. Both types of films cater to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
