<p><strong>The Differential For Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Differential</li><li>Lock Differential</li><li>Limited Slip Differential</li><li>Electronic Differential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open Differential: Allows the wheels to rotate at different speeds to prevent binding when turning. Common in everyday passenger vehicles for smooth driving.</p><p>Lock Differential: Locks the wheels together for increased traction in off-road or low traction situations. Common in 4x4 and off-road vehicles.</p><p>Limited Slip Differential: Controls the power distribution between wheels to reduce wheel spin and improve traction. Common in performance and sports cars.</p><p>Electronic Differential: Utilizes sensors and electronics to constantly adjust power distribution for optimal traction and handling. Common in high-performance and luxury vehicles.</p></p>
