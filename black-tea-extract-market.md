<p><p>Black tea extract is available in two main types within the market: hot water soluble and cold water soluble. Hot water soluble black tea extract dissolves easily in hot water, making it suitable for use in hot beverages like tea and coffee. On the other hand, cold water soluble black tea extract can easily dissolve in cold water, making it ideal for use in cold beverages like iced tea or smoothies. These two types cater to different preferences and usage scenarios, providing versatility and convenience for consumers.</p></p>
