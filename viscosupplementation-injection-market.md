<p><p>Viscosupplementation injection is a medical procedure used to treat osteoarthritis by injecting a thick fluid into the joints to lubricate and cushion them. This procedure is mainly used in knee joints but can also be used in other joints like hips, shoulders, and ankles.</p><p>The viscosupplementation injection market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The rising prevalence of osteoarthritis, along with the increasing elderly population, is driving the market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on developing more efficient and long-lasting products to meet the growing demand for osteoarthritis treatment.</p><p>Another major trend in the market is the increasing preference for minimally invasive procedures. Viscosupplementation injection offers a less invasive alternative to surgeries, providing pain relief and improving joint function, with fewer risks and complications.</p><p>Regionally, North America dominates the viscosupplementation injection market due to the high prevalence of arthritis and the presence of advanced healthcare infrastructure.
