<p><p>3D Laser Sorting Machine market includes various types such as Belt Sorting Machine and Rotary Disc Sorting Machine. The Belt Sorting Machine is designed with a conveyor belt system to sort objects based on size, color, and shape using 3D laser technology. On the other hand, the Rotary Disc Sorting Machine consists of rotating discs with sensors to accurately detect and sort objects in a fast and efficient manner. Both types offer advanced sorting capabilities and are widely used in industries such as food processing, recycling, and manufacturing.</p></p>
