<p><strong>The Electric Podiatry Examination Chairs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Section</li><li>3-Section</li><li>4-Section</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric podiatry examination chairs market consists of three types: 2-section, 3-section, and 4-section chairs. A 2-section chair provides two adjustable sections for patients' back and legs. A 3-section chair includes three sections: headrest, backrest, and lower leg support that can be adjusted independently. Lastly, a 4-section chair offers four adjustable sections: headrest, backrest, seat, and footrest, allowing for even more flexibility in positioning. These various types cater to different podiatry requirements, providing comfort and convenience for both patients and practitioners.</p></p>
