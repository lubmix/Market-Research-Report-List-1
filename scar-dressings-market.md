<p><p>Scar dressings are used to promote wound healing and minimize scarring. Non-sterile dressings refer to those without guaranteed sterility, usually used for minor wounds at home. While non-sterile dressings are commonly available in retail stores, sterile dressings are typically distributed by medical suppliers or necessary in clinical settings. Both types serve the purpose of protecting wounds and preventing infection, but sterile dressings offer a higher level of safety.</p></p>
