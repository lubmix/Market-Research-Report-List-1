<p><p>Online Admissions Software market types include cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based solutions are web-based platforms hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage admissions software through internet connectivity. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as software updates and maintenance are handled by the provider. On-premises solutions, on the other hand, require installation and maintenance of the software on local servers. They offer complete control and data security but lack the flexibility and scalability advantages provided by cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
