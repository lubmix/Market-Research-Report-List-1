<p><strong>The Spray Foam Insulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Cell Foam</li><li>Closed-Cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray foam insulation market consists of two main types: open-cell foam and closed-cell foam. Open-cell foam is less dense and is suitable for interior applications where moisture control is not a major concern. Closed-cell foam is denser and provides a moisture barrier, making it ideal for exterior use and areas prone to high humidity. Both types offer excellent thermal insulation properties, energy efficiency, and air sealing capabilities, making them popular choices for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
