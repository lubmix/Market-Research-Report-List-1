<p><p>The Medical Electronic Nose market is segmented into five main types: Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS), Conducting Polymer (CP), Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW), and Others. MOS sensors detect gases based on changes in electrical resistance, CP sensors use changes in conductivity, QCM sensors detect mass changes on a quartz crystal, and SAW sensors measure changes in frequency caused by gas interactions. Other types may include optical sensors or bio-electronic sensors. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in medical diagnostics and monitoring.</p></p>
