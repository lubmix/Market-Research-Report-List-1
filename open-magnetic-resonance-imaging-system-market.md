<p><strong>The Open Magnetic Resonance Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Superconducting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Open Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) System market comprises two types: Permanent Magnet and Superconducting. Permanent Magnet systems use a fixed magnet made of magnetic materials, such as iron, which produces a constant magnetic field. Superconducting systems, on the other hand, utilize magnets cooled to extremely low temperatures, enabling them to generate stronger magnetic fields. These systems offer patients more comfort with their open design, allowing a greater feeling of space during imaging procedures. Both types provide high-quality images in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.</p></p>
