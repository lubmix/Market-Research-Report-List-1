<p><p>Polyester boards come in various types, including Polyester MDF, Polyester Plywood, and Polyester Blockboard. Polyester MDF boards are made from wood fibers bonded together with polyester resin, offering a smooth surface and high durability. Polyester Plywood is made by bonding multiple layers of wood veneers with polyester resin, providing a strong and stable material. Polyester Blockboard consists of a core made of wooden blocks enclosed in polyester-coated veneers, offering a lightweight and sturdy option for furniture and construction projects.</p></p>
