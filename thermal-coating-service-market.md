<p><strong>The Thermal Coating Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flame Spray</li><li>Plasma Spray</li><li>Metallizing Spary</li><li>Electric Arc Spary</li><li>HVOF (High-Velocity Oxy Fuel) Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal coating services are used to protect surfaces from heat, corrosion, and wear. Flame spray involves heating and projecting a wire or powder onto a surface. Plasma spray uses a high-temperature plasma arc to melt and propel coating materials. Metallizing spray involves using a flame or electric arc to melt wire or powder and project it onto a surface. Electric arc spray uses an electric arc to melt wire and spray it onto a surface. HVOF spray involves heating and accelerating a gas-fuel mixture to create a high-velocity spray for coating surfaces.</p></p>
