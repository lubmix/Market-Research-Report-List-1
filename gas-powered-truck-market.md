<p><strong>The Gas Powered Truck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CNG Vehicle</li><li>LNG Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas-powered truck market consists of two main types: CNG vehicles and LNG vehicles. CNG vehicles run on compressed natural gas, which is stored in high-pressure tanks on the vehicle. These vehicles are more common and have a wider infrastructure for refueling. LNG vehicles, on the other hand, run on liquefied natural gas, which is stored at very low temperatures. While LNG vehicles have a longer driving range and higher energy density compared to CNG vehicles, they require specialized refueling stations and storage facilities.</p></p>
