<p><p>Solvent-free packaging adhesives are available in two main market types: one-component and two-component. Two-component adhesives require mixing of two separate components before application, allowing for greater flexibility in terms of adjusting curing times and properties. Both types of adhesives offer environmentally friendly solutions for packaging applications, eliminating the need for harmful solvents while providing strong and durable bond strength.</p></p>
