<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Earthmoving equipment tires come in two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are constructed with steel belts that run perpendicular to the tread, providing better traction and stability. These tires have a longer lifespan and offer better fuel efficiency. On the other hand, bias tires have a diagonal ply construction, making them more suitable for rough terrains. They are cost-effective and provide a smoother ride but have a shorter lifespan compared to radial tires. Both types cater to different needs in the earthmoving industry.</p></p>
