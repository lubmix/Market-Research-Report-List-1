<p><strong>The Car Sharing Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded</li><li>Tethered</li><li>Integrated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded car sharing telematics involve a built-in system within the vehicle that enables communication between the car and the car sharing platform. Tethered telematics requires a physical connection to the vehicle, such as a dongle that is plugged into the car's diagnostic port. Integrated telematics combines the embedded and tethered systems to provide a seamless and advanced communication interface between the vehicle and the car sharing platform. Each type offers different levels of integration and functionality for car sharing services.</p></p>
