<p><p>Donor egg in vitro fertilization (IVF) services involve the use of donated eggs from another woman to facilitate pregnancy in individuals or couples struggling with infertility. Donor eggs are typically obtained from young and healthy women who have undergone thorough medical and genetic screening.</p><p>The donor egg IVF services market has been experiencing significant growth over the years, driven by factors such as increasing infertility rates, advancements in assisted reproductive technologies, and growing awareness and acceptance of the procedure.
