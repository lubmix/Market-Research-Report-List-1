<p><p>Nucleic acid-based therapeutics market includes various types of therapies such as anti-sense and anti-gene, short inhibitory sequences, gene transfer therapy, nucleoside analogs, ribozymes, and aptamers. Anti-sense and anti-gene therapies involve interference with gene expression, while short inhibitory sequences target specific genes for suppression. Gene transfer therapy involves introducing new genes into cells to treat diseases. Nucleoside analogs mimic nucleotides to disrupt DNA replication. Ribozymes are RNA molecules that can catalyze chemical reactions, and aptamers are single-stranded DNA or RNA molecules that bind to specific target molecules.</p></p>
