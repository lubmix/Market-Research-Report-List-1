<p><p>The elbow wrist hand orthoses market is classified into two types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are flexible and adjustable devices that use various mechanisms to support and assist the movement of the elbow, wrist, and hand. They provide dynamic support and are often used in conditions requiring rehabilitation or assistance in functional activities. On the other hand, static orthotics are rigid devices that aim to immobilize or stabilize the affected area. They are commonly used in cases where stability and limited movement are desired, such as fractures or severe sprains.</p></p>
