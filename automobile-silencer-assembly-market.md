<p><ul><li>Absorption Silencer</li><li>Reflection Silencer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile Silencer Assembly market consists of two main types: Absorption Silencer and Reflection Silencer. Absorption Silencer is designed to absorb and dissipate sound waves through sound-deadening materials, such as fibrous materials or insulation. On the other hand, Reflection Silencer works by reflecting sound waves and causing them to cancel each other out. Both types aim to reduce noise emissions from the vehicle's exhaust system. Absorption Silencers are more commonly used due to their effectiveness in reducing noise levels.</p></p>
