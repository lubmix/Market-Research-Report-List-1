<p><p>The conventional rear-view mirror is a traditional design that uses reflective glass to allow the driver to see what is behind their vehicle. This type of mirror often includes features like auto-dimming, blind spot detection, and even video recording capabilities. Both types serve the same purpose of enhancing visibility for the driver, but the smart rear-view mirror offers additional technological advancements.</p></p>
