PVC clamshells are known for their durability and clarity, while PET clamshells offer good barrier properties. Polystyrene clamshells are lightweight and cost-effective, making them popular for packaging purposes. ABS clamshells are known for their impact resistance and can withstand harsh environmental conditions. Each type of plastic clamshell packaging offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
