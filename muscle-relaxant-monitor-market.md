<p><p>Muscle Relaxant Monitors are used to measure the degree of muscle relaxation during surgery. EMG Type Muscle Relaxant Monitors use electromyography to monitor muscle activity, while MMG Type Muscle Relaxant Monitors use mechanomyography. EMG monitors are more commonly used and provide real-time feedback on muscle activity, while MMG monitors are newer technology that measures muscle response to stimuli. Both types of monitors are essential tools for anesthesiologists and healthcare professionals to ensure optimal muscle relaxation during surgical procedures.</p></p>
