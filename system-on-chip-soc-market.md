<p><p>System on Chip (SoC) are integrated circuits that combine multiple components like processors, memory, and peripherals into a single chip. The types of SoC markets are divided into digital, analog, mixed signal, and others. Digital SoCs mainly focus on processing and data manipulation, while analog SoCs primarily handle continuous signals like sound and video. Mixed signal SoCs integrate both digital and analog components. Other types of SoCs cater to specific niche applications such as radio frequency or power management.</p></p>
