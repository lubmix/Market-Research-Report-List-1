<p><p>Solvent-free polyurethane adhesives are typically categorized into two main types: monocomponent and bi-component. Monocomponent adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient and easy to apply without the need for complex mixing. Bi-component adhesives require two separate components to be mixed together prior to application, offering greater control over the adhesive's properties and allowing for customization based on specific application requirements. Both types of adhesive are popular in the market for their environmental friendliness and high performance.</p></p>
