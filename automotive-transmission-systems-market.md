<p><p>Automotive transmission systems can be broadly categorized into different types based on their mechanisms and functions. Both systems play a crucial role in determining the performance and fuel efficiency of vehicles, with manual transmissions offering greater control to the driver and double clutch transmissions providing smoother and faster gear changes.</p></p>
