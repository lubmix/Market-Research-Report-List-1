<p><p>The automotive pop-up sunroof market is divided into two main types: power sunroof and manual sunroof. Power sunroofs are operated electronically, allowing for easy opening and closing with the push of a button. They often come with additional features such as auto-closing in case of rain. Manual sunroofs, on the other hand, are operated manually by turning a handle. While they are less convenient than power sunroofs, they are often more affordable and easier to maintain. Both types offer drivers the ability to let in natural light and fresh air while driving.</p></p>
