<p><p>Waterproofing cement lining market can be classified into one component and two component types. One component lining products are pre-mixed materials that require only water for application, making them easy to use and cost-effective. On the other hand, two component linings require mixing two separate components before application, offering better performance and durability. Both options provide effective waterproofing solutions for various applications, with two component linings being preferred for projects that require higher levels of protection and longevity.</p></p>
