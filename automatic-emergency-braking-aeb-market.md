<p><p>Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB) is a safety technology that helps prevent crashes by automatically applying the brakes if the driver fails to do so in time. There are three main types of AEB systems: Forward Collision Warning, which alerts the driver of an imminent collision, Dynamic Brake Support, which provides additional braking power to help the driver stop in time, and Crash Imminent Braking, which automatically applies the brakes if a crash is unavoidable. These systems work together to increase safety on the roads.</p></p>
