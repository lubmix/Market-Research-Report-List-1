<p><p>The Robotic End-of-Arm Tools market can be classified into four main types based on their operation mechanism: Electrical, Hydraulical, Mechanical, and Pneumatical. Electrical End-of-Arm Tools utilize electric motors for precise movements and control. Hydraulical tools use hydraulic fluid to power the tool for heavy-duty applications. Mechanical tools rely on mechanical components for gripping and manipulation tasks. Pneumatical tools use compressed air to operate, offering fast and efficient performance. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different industrial applications.</p></p>
