<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) Communication Market includes various components such as Transmitters, Receivers, Modulators, Demodulators, Encoders, and Decoders. Transmitters are used to send optical signals, while Receivers capture these signals. Modulators and Demodulators help in modulating and demodulating the signal for transmission and reception, respectively. Encoders convert data into a specific format for transmission, and Decoders decode the received data into its original form. These components play a crucial role in enabling high-speed wireless communication through optical technology.</p></p>
