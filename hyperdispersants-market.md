There are two main types of hyperdispersants market: Water-Borne Dispersants and Solvent-Borne Dispersants. Water-Borne Dispersants are environmentally friendly and widely used in industries where water-based formulations are preferred. On the other hand, Solvent-Borne Dispersants are used when water-based formulations are not suitable, offering better solvent compatibility. Both types of hyperdispersants play a crucial role in improving the stability and performance of products in various industries.</p></p>
