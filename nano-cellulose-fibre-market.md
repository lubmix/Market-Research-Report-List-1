<p><p>Nano-Cellulose Fibre market can be categorized into Cellulose Nano Fibrils (CNF), Cellulose Nano Crystals (CNC), and Bacterial Nanocellulose (BNC). CNF is long and entangled fibrils, CNC is rod-like particles with high aspect ratio, and BNC is produced by bacteria cultured on a growth medium. These different types of nano-cellulose fibres have various applications in industries such as composites, textiles, packaging, and biomedical, due to their unique properties like high strength, lightweight, and biodegradability.</p></p>
