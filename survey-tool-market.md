<p><p>The survey tool market can be categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. In contrast, Cloud-Based survey tools operate via the internet, allowing users to access and utilize the tools on a subscription basis. This offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of use, as the tools are managed by the service provider and can be accessed from any device with an internet connection.</p></p>
