<p><p>Rubber-based flexible foam market can be classified into different types based on the type of rubber used. NBR-based flexible foam is known for its oil and chemical resistance, making it suitable for industrial applications. EPDM-based foam provides excellent weather and UV resistance, making it ideal for outdoor applications. Chloroprene-based foam offers good resistance to weather and ozone, making it suitable for automotive and construction industries. Other types of rubber-based foams include silicone and natural rubber, each offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
