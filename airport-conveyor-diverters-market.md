<p><p>Airport conveyor diverters are devices used to redirect baggage or cargo on a conveyor belt in airport distribution systems. There are three main types of conveyor diverters in the market: horizontal, vertical, and modular. Horizontal diverters move items left or right on the same plane, vertical diverters lift items up and down to different levels, and modular diverters are flexible systems that can be customized based on specific airport layouts. Each type has its own unique benefits and applications in airport logistics.</p></p>
