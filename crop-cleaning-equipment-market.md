<p><p>Crop cleaning equipment is categorized into two main types - Pre-Cleaning Type and Fine Cleaning Type. Pre-Cleaning Type equipment is designed to remove large impurities like stones, stalks, and leaves from the harvested crop before it goes through further processing. Fine Cleaning Type equipment, on the other hand, is used to thoroughly clean the crop by removing smaller impurities such as dust, broken seeds, and other debris. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and purity of the final products.</p></p>
