<p><p>The Automotive Split-view Camera Module market offers two main types of camera modules based on the type of sensor chip used: CCD Chip and CMOS Chip. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) chips offer high-quality images with low noise levels, making them suitable for applications requiring high resolution and sensitivity. On the other hand, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) chips are more cost-effective and energy-efficient, making them popular for a wide range of automotive camera applications. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
