<p><p>Data Management System (DBMS) market comprises of two main types: Relational and Non-Relational. Relational databases store data in rows and columns, and use structured query language (SQL) for managing and querying data. They are widely used in traditional business applications. Non-Relational databases, on the other hand, store data in a more flexible manner and do not require a fixed schema like relational databases. They are commonly used in big data and real-time applications where scalability and flexibility are key requirements.</p></p>
