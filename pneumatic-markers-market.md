<p><p>Pneumatic markers are divided into two main types: pneumatic benchtop markers and pneumatic portable markers. Pneumatic benchtop markers are stationary machines typically used in manufacturing facilities for marking products on a production line. On the other hand, pneumatic portable markers are handheld devices that can be easily carried around for marking products in various locations. Both types of pneumatic markers use compressed air to create markings on a variety of surfaces.</p></p>
