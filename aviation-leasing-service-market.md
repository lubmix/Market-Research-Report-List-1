<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aviation Leasing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Lease</li><li>Dry Lease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation leasing service market consists of two main types: wet lease and dry lease. In a wet lease, the lessor (leasing company) provides the aircraft along with the crew, maintenance, and insurance to the lessee (airline) for a specific period. This arrangement is typically used by airlines for short-term needs or during peak seasons. On the other hand, in a dry lease, the lessor provides only the aircraft, and the lessee is responsible for all other operating aspects, such as crew, maintenance, and insurance. Dry leases are usually long-term agreements and allow airlines more flexibility and control.</p></p>
