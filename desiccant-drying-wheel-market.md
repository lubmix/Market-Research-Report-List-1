<p><p>Desiccant drying wheels are commonly categorized into three main types: Silica gel, Molecular sieve, and Other desiccants. Silica gel desiccants are known for their high adsorption capacity and ability to maintain a low moisture level. Molecular sieve desiccants offer a more selective adsorption process, ideal for removing specific impurities. Other desiccants, such as activated alumina or clay, cater to specific applications requiring unique adsorption properties. Each type of desiccant drying wheel serves a distinct purpose in moisture control and air purification processes.</p></p>
