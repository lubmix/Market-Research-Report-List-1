<p><strong>The Low Noise Oscillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage-Controlled</li><li>Temperature-Compensated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voltage-Controlled Oscillator (VCO) is a type of oscillator whose output frequency can be controlled by varying the input voltage. It is widely used in frequency synthesizers, phase-locked loops, and communication systems.</p><p>Temperature-Compensated Oscillator (TCXO) is designed to have stable output frequency over a wide temperature range. These oscillators are commonly used in applications where temperature fluctuations can impact performance, such as GPS receivers and cellular base stations.</p><p>Other types of low noise oscillators include oven-controlled oscillators (OCXO), surface acoustic wave oscillators (SAWO), and digitally controlled oscillators (DCO). These oscillators offer different features and benefits depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
