<p><ul><li>Pleated</li><li>String Wound</li><li>Melt-blown</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pre-filtration filter cartridges are commonly categorized into four main types - pleated, string wound, melt-blown, and others. Pleated cartridges have a large surface area for improved filtration efficiency. String wound cartridges use tightly wound fibers to trap contaminants. Melt-blown cartridges use a process where fibers are melted and blown onto a core to create a dense filter material. Other types of pre-filtration cartridges may include carbon block or ceramic filters, each offering unique filtration properties for specific applications.</p></p>
