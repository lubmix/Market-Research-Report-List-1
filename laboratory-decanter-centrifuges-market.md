<p><p>Laboratory Decanter Centrifuges come in two main types: Vertical Decanter Centrifuges and Horizontal Decanter Centrifuges. Vertical Decanter Centrifuges are designed with the bowl axis positioned vertically, allowing for efficient separation of solids and liquids. On the other hand, Horizontal Decanter Centrifuges have the bowl axis positioned horizontally, enabling continuous separation of materials. Both types of centrifuges are widely used in laboratory settings for various applications such as chemical processing, pharmaceutical research, and waste water treatment.</p></p>
