<p><p>The Surgical Imaging Display market includes various types of display technologies. Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) displays use electron beams to create images, but they are bulky and consume more power. Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) screens use liquid crystals to show images and are lightweight but may have limited viewing angles. Light Emitting Diode (LED) displays are energy-efficient and offer better brightness and contrast. Three-dimensional (3D) displays provide a realistic depth perception. Other display types in the market may include OLED, QLED, or MicroLED, each with its own unique features and benefits.</p></p>
