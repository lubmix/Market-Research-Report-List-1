<p><p>Fifth-wheel hitches come in two main types: 2-way pivot head and 4-way pivot head. The 2-way pivot head allows for movement in two directions, front to back and side to side, providing greater flexibility when towing. On the other hand, the 4-way pivot head allows for movement in four directions, including up and down, in addition to front to back and side to side movements. This design offers even more control and maneuverability while towing heavy loads, making it a popular choice among RV and trailer owners.</p></p>
