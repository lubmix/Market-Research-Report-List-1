<p><p>Wastewater Treatment and Reuse market types consist of biological, mechanical, and chemical treatment. Biological treatment involves the use of microorganisms to break down organic matter in wastewater. Mechanical treatment involves physical processes like sedimentation, filtration, and aeration to remove solids and contaminants from the water. Chemical treatment utilizes chemicals like chlorine or ozone to disinfect and remove pollutants from the wastewater. These different treatment methods provide diverse options for wastewater treatment and reuse, allowing for the purification and safe disposal or reuse of wastewater.</p></p>
