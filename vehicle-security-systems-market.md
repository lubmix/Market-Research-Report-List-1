<p><strong>The Vehicle Security Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alarm</li><li>Immobilizer</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry</li><li>Passive Keyless Entry</li><li>Central Locking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle security systems market can be divided into five main types: alarms, immobilizers, remote keyless entry systems, passive keyless entry systems, and central locking systems. Alarms trigger a loud alert in case of unauthorized access. Immobilizers prevent the engine from starting without the correct key or code. Remote keyless entry systems allow for locking and unlocking of the vehicle from a distance. Passive keyless entry systems unlock the vehicle when the key fob is in proximity. Central locking systems enable the locking and unlocking of all vehicle doors simultaneously.</p></p>
