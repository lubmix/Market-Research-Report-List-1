<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Vision Cameras market includes different types of cameras such as Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture a two-dimensional image in a single shot, making them suitable for capturing still images or videos of stationary objects. On the other hand, Line Scan Cameras capture images line by line, making them ideal for inspecting continuous objects like conveyor belts. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare for quality control, inspection, and surveillance purposes.</p></p>
