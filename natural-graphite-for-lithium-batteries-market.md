<p><p>Natural graphite for lithium batteries is available in two main types - Crystalline Graphite and Amorphous (Implicit Crystalline) Graphite. Crystalline Graphite is a highly structured form of graphite that is composed of distinct layers of graphite crystals, making it ideal for use in lithium batteries due to its high capacity for energy storage. On the other hand, Implicit Crystalline Graphite lacks the distinct layering of crystalline graphite but still offers excellent performance in lithium batteries.</p></p>
