<p><p>GERD (Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease) market types are categorized into different treatment options. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) are medications that reduce stomach acid production, providing long-term relief from GERD symptoms. H2 Receptor Antagonists block the production of acid and provide short-term relief. Antacids neutralize stomach acid and offer temporary relief from heartburn. Prokinetics help strengthen the lower esophageal sphincter and improve gastric emptying. The Complementary and Alternative Medicine (CAM) market includes natural remedies, such as herbal supplements or acupuncture, that aim to alleviate GERD symptoms.</p></p>
