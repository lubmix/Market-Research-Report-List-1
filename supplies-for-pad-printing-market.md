<p><p>Supplies for pad printing market primarily consist of inks, pads, plates, and other related accessories. Inks play a vital role as the printing medium, transferring the design onto the desired surface. Pads, made from silicone or rubber, are responsible for picking up the ink from the plate and transferring it to the object being printed. Plates, usually made of metal or polymer, hold the design to be transferred. Other supplies include cleaning agents, adhesive tapes, solvent cups, and storage systems, which are crucial for maintaining the quality and efficiency of the pad printing process.</p></p>
