<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vehicle Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle brake fluid market is categorized into four main types- DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is glycol-based and commonly used in most vehicles. DOT 4 has higher boiling point than DOT 3 and is used in high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles. DOT 5 is silicone-based and not compatible with other types. DOT 5.1 is an improved version of DOT 3 and DOT 4, with even higher boiling point. Each type is designed for specific vehicles and performance requirements.</p></p>
