<p><p>Spray desuperheaters are specialized equipment used to reduce the temperature of superheated steam in industrial processes. They come in two main types: Single Nozzle and Multiple Nozzle. Single Nozzle desuperheaters have a simpler design and are typically used in smaller applications where precise control is not critical. Multiple Nozzle desuperheaters, on the other hand, have multiple injection points for better control over temperature reduction and are used in larger industrial settings where precise temperature control is essential.</p></p>
