<p><p>Loose absorbent markets can be categorized into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic absorbents are made from natural materials such as plant fibers, corn cobs, or sawdust, which are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. In contrast, inorganic absorbents are usually composed of synthetic materials like clay or silica gel, which are often more effective at absorbing liquids but may not be as eco-friendly. Both types of absorbents are commonly used for cleaning up spills in various industrial settings.</p></p>
