<p><p>Ischemic Stroke Diagnostic Equipment market is divided into several types based on the cause of the stroke. Thrombotic stroke occurs when a blood clot forms in one of the brain's arteries, obstructing the blood flow. Embolic stroke happens when a blood clot or plaque travels from another part of the body and blocks a brain artery. The "Others" category includes less common causes such as a clot forming in a small blood vessel or a narrowing of the blood vessels supplying the brain. These types help in identifying and diagnosing strokes accurately.</p></p>
