<p><p>Polyethylene Coated Paperboard for Packaging is primarily classified into two types based on the type of polyethylene used: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LDPE is a commonly used type of polyethylene that offers flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for flexible packaging applications. On the other hand, HDPE is known for its stiffness and strength, making it ideal for applications requiring higher tensile strength and rigidity. Both types are widely used in the packaging industry for their unique properties and benefits.</p></p>
