<p><p>Egg steamers come in two main types: single layer and multilayer egg steamers. Single layer egg steamers are designed to cook a smaller quantity of eggs at once, typically up to 7-8 eggs. On the other hand, multilayer egg steamers feature multiple trays or layers, allowing users to cook a larger quantity of eggs at one time, usually up to 14-16 eggs. Multilayer egg steamers are ideal for households or commercial settings where a larger quantity of eggs need to be cooked quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
