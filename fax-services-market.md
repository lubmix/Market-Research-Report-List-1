<p><p>The fax services market can be classified into three main types: On-premises fax solutions, cloud-based fax services, and hybrid fax solutions. On-premises fax solutions involve setting up fax infrastructure on-site, requiring hardware, software, and maintenance. Cloud-based fax services utilize internet-based platforms to send and receive faxes securely, eliminating the need for physical infrastructure. Hybrid fax solutions combine the benefits of on-premises and cloud-based systems, allowing organizations to leverage existing resources while accessing additional features and scalability in the cloud.</p></p>
