<p><p>Aircraft & Aerospace Composite Panels market includes two main types - Sandwich Panels and High Temperature Panels. </p><p>Sandwich Panels consist of two thin yet strong outer layers, usually made of carbon fiber or fiberglass, with a lightweight core material in between. These panels offer a good balance of strength and weight, making them ideal for aircraft structures.</p><p>High Temperature Panels are designed to withstand extreme heat conditions, typically found in aerospace applications such as engine components. They are made of advanced materials that can maintain their properties at high temperatures, ensuring reliable performance in challenging environments.</p></p>
