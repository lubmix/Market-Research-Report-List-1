<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) is a versatile polymer used in various industries, with three main types dominating the market: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE offers high strength and durability, making it suitable for products like pipes and containers. LDPE is flexible and transparent, commonly used in packaging films. LLDPE combines the properties of HDPE and LDPE, offering high tensile strength and puncture resistance, making it ideal for stretch wraps and industrial applications.</p></p>
