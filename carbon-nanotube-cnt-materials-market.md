<p><strong>The Carbon Nanotube (CNT) Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SWNTs</li><li>MWNTs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs) are divided into two main types based on their structure: Single-Walled Nanotubes (SWNTs) and Multi-Walled Nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs consist of a single cylindrical layer of carbon atoms, while MWNTs consist of multiple concentric layers. SWNTs are known for their exceptional strength and electrical conductivity, making them ideal for applications such as electronics and aerospace. On the other hand, MWNTs are favored for their superior mechanical properties and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for reinforcing materials in composites and batteries.</p></p>
