<p><p>Engineered TPU market consists of two main types: foam and elastomer. Foam TPU is characterized by its lightweight and cushioning properties, making it ideal for applications such as insoles, padding, and insulation. Elastomer TPU, on the other hand, is known for its flexibility and durability, making it suitable for use in gaskets, seals, and automotive components. Both foam and elastomer TPU offer a wide range of benefits and can be tailored to specific applications based on their unique physical properties.</p></p>
