<p><p>Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) is a technology that aims to capture carbon dioxide emissions produced during the combustion of fossil fuels to prevent them from entering the atmosphere. The different types of CCS market include Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture, which captures CO2 before combustion, Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture, which burns fuel in oxygen instead of air to produce a concentrated stream of CO2, and Post-Combustion Carbon Capture, which captures CO2 from the flue gas after combustion. These technologies play a critical role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating climate change.</p></p>
