<p><p>Document scanners are classified into three main types based on the technology used to capture images - CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), CIS (Contact Image Sensor), and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD scanners are known for their high image quality and accuracy, making them suitable for professional use. CIS scanners are more compact and energy-efficient, making them ideal for personal and home use. CMOS scanners offer a balance between image quality and speed, making them popular for various scanning applications. Each type has its own strengths and is chosen based on specific user needs.</p></p>
