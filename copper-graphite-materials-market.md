<p><p>Copper graphite materials are commonly classified into two types based on their physical form: powder and blocky. Powder copper graphite materials are finely ground particles that can be easily pressed into various shapes and sizes. They are often used in applications requiring high conductivity and thermal resistance. On the other hand, blocky copper graphite materials are solid blocks or chunks that are typically used in more heavy-duty applications that require durability and strength. Both forms of copper graphite materials play a crucial role in various industries and applications.</p></p>
