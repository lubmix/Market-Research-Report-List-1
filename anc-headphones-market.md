<p><p>ANC headphones come in three main types - in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear. In-ear ANC headphones are small, lightweight, and fit directly into the ear canal. Over-ear ANC headphones have cushioned ear cups that completely cover the ears, offering the best noise cancellation and audio quality but are bulkier in size. Each type caters to different preferences and needs, allowing consumers to choose the one that best suits their lifestyle.</p></p>
