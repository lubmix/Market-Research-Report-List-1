<p><p>Ceramic capillary market types include bonding capillary and wedge capillary. Bonding capillaries are used for joining different materials together, typically metals or ceramics. They provide a strong bond due to their high temperature resistance and chemical stability. Wedge capillaries, on the other hand, are used for sealing applications, especially in the medical and electronics industries. They provide a reliable seal due to their precise and uniform structure. Both types of capillaries play a crucial role in various industries for joining and sealing applications.</p></p>
