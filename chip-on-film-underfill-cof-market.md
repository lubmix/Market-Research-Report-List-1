<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Chip On Film Underfill (COF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill (NUF)</li><li>Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) Underfill</li><li>Non-Conductive Film (NCF) Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill (MUF) Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip on Film Underfill (COF) market includes various types of underfill techniques such as Capillary Underfill (CUF) which uses capillary action to fill the gap between chip and substrate, No Flow Underfill (NUF) which involves pre-applying underfill material before chip placement, Non-Conductive Paste (NCP) Underfill for electrical insulation, Non-Conductive Film (NCF) Underfill for protection against physical damage, and Molded Underfill (MUF) which involves molding the underfill material around the chip. These techniques cater to different requirements in the COF market.</p></p>
