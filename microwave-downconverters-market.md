<p><p>Microwave downconverters are utilized in various frequency bands for communication and radar applications. Ka-band downconverters function in the 26-40 GHz range, and K-band downconverters operate in the 18-26 GHz range. Other frequency bands such as X-band and C-band also have downconverters designed for specific applications. Each frequency band serves different purposes and may require specialized equipment for optimal performance in different communication and radar systems.</p></p>
