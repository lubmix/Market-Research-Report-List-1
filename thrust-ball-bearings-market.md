Single-Direction Bearings are designed to support axial loads in one direction, while Double-Direction Bearings can support axial loads in both directions. Single-Direction Bearings are commonly used in applications where the load is primarily in one direction, while Double-Direction Bearings are ideal for applications where the load may change direction.
