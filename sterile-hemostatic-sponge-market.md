<p><p>Sterile hemostatic sponges are widely available in the market in different types including gelatin, chitosan, collagen, and other variations. Gelatin sponges are derived from animal sources and help in creating a blood clot at the site of injury. Chitosan sponges are made from the exoskeletons of crustaceans and have antimicrobial properties. Collagen sponges are produced from animal connective tissues and promote wound healing. Other variations of hemostatic sponges may include synthetic materials or plant-derived substances. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for different medical applications.</p></p>
