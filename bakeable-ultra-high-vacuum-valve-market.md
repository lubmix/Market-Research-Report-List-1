<p><p>The bakeable ultra-high vacuum valve market includes two main types: gate valves and angle valves. Gate valves are designed for primarily on-off service and are commonly used in vacuum systems for their low leak rates. Angle valves, on the other hand, are used for throttling applications and feature a 90-degree angle between the inlet and outlet ports. Both types are crucial in creating and maintaining high vacuum conditions in various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
