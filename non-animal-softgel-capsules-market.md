<p><ul><li>Plant Polysaccharides Softgel Capsules</li><li>Starch Softgel Capsules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-animal softgel capsules market includes two types: plant polysaccharides softgel capsules and starch softgel capsules. Plant polysaccharides softgel capsules are made from natural plant-based materials such as cellulose and carrageenan. They are suitable for vegetarians and offer a vegan alternative to traditional gelatin-based capsules. Starch softgel capsules, on the other hand, are made from starch derived from sources like corn or potato. They provide a gluten-free option and are often used in pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries as an alternative to gelatin capsules.</p></p>
