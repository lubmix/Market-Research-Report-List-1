<p><p>The Glass Vaccine Vial market consists of two main types: Molded Glass Vial and Tubular Glass Vial. Molded Glass Vials are produced by molding molten glass into the desired shape, making them more resistant to breakage and leakage. Tubular Glass Vials, on the other hand, are made by drawing molten glass into a tube-like structure and then cutting it into appropriate lengths. These vials are known for their superior clarity and are often used in applications where visibility and accurate measurement of the contents are crucial, such as vaccine packaging.</p></p>
