<p><p>Scrubber driers are categorized into two main types: walk-behind scrubber driers and stand-on/ride-on scrubber driers. Walk-behind scrubber driers are ideal for smaller spaces and provide the operator with more control and precision in cleaning. Stand-on/ride-on scrubber driers, on the other hand, are larger machines that are suitable for cleaning larger areas quickly and efficiently. These types of scrubber driers are often used in commercial and industrial settings where there is a high volume of foot traffic.</p></p>
