<p><strong>The Polyethylene Fuel Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-density Crosslinked Polyethylene (XLPE) Tanks</li><li>Linear Polyethylene Tanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene fuel tanks come in two main types: High-density Crosslinked Polyethylene (XLPE) tanks and Linear Polyethylene tanks. XLPE tanks are known for their superior strength and resistance to chemicals and impacts, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Linear Polyethylene tanks are more lightweight and cost-effective, making them a popular choice for smaller vehicles or applications where weight is a concern. Both types offer durability and corrosion resistance, making them a reliable choice for fuel storage.</p></p>
