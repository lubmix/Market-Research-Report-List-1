<p><strong>The Cement Admixtures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mineral Admixture</li><li>Chemical Admixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cement admixtures are additives used in cement and concrete mixtures to enhance their properties. Mineral admixtures are derived from natural sources such as fly ash, silica fume, and slag, which improve workability, strength, and durability of concrete. Chemical admixtures, on the other hand, are synthetic additives like water reducers, accelerators, and air-entraining agents that modify the properties of cement mixtures. Both types of admixtures play a crucial role in the construction industry by offering a wide range of benefits to concrete structures.</p></p>
