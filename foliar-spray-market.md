<p><p>Foliar spray market types include nitrogenous, phosphatic, potassic, and micronutrients. Nitrogenous foliar sprays provide plants with essential nitrogen, promoting leaf growth and overall health. Phosphatic foliar sprays aid in root development and energy transfer within the plant. Potassic foliar sprays contribute to fruit and flower production, as well as overall plant strength. Micronutrients foliar sprays provide plants with essential trace elements necessary for optimal growth and development. Each type of foliar spray plays a vital role in enhancing plant health and productivity.</p></p>
