<p><p>The Floating Bollard Market is divided into two types, Type I and Type II. Type I floating bollards are designed for marine environments with high levels of wave action and strong currents. They are typically larger in size and feature advanced technology to withstand challenging conditions. On the other hand, Type II floating bollards are more suited for calmer waters and lighter usage. They are generally smaller and simpler in design, providing basic protection and mooring capabilities for vessels.</p></p>
