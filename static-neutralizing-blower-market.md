<p><p>Static neutralizing blowers are essential devices used to eliminate static electricity from surfaces in various industries. There are two main types of static neutralizing blowers in the market: active and passive. Active static neutralizing blowers require a power source to generate ions that neutralize static charges, while passive static neutralizing blowers utilize natural airflow to neutralize static charges. Both types are effective in reducing static electricity on surfaces, but the choice between them depends on the specific application and environment.</p></p>
