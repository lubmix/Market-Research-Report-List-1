<p><p>Filter bonding adhesives market includes different types based on the materials used. Epoxy resins adhesives provide strong and durable bonds, ideal for high-performance applications. Polyurethanes adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to moisture and chemicals. Thermoplastic adhesives are heat-activated and provide a strong bond that can be easily reformed. Plastisols adhesives are PVC-based and offer good adhesion to various surfaces. Cyanoacrylates adhesives, commonly known as super glue, provide fast curing and strong bonds for quick applications. Each type caters to specific needs in the filter bonding industry.</p></p>
