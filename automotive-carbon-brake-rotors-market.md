<p><p>The automotive carbon brake rotors market includes two main types of materials: carbon-carbon matrix and carbon-ceramic matrix. Carbon-carbon matrix brake rotors are made from carbon fibers suspended in a matrix of carbon, providing exceptional heat resistance and durability. On the other hand, carbon-ceramic matrix brake rotors combine carbon fibers with a ceramic matrix to enhance heat dissipation and reduce weight. Both types offer significant advantages in terms of performance and longevity compared to traditional brake rotor materials.</p></p>
