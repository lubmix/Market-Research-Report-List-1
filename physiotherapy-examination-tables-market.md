<p><p>Physiotherapy examination tables come in three main types: electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric tables allow for easy adjustment using a motorized system, providing convenience for both the patient and the therapist. Manual tables require physical effort to adjust the height and position. Hydraulic tables use a hydraulic system for smooth and controlled adjustments. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and preferences of the physiotherapy clinic or practitioner.</p></p>
