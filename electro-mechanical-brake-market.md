<p><strong>The Electro-mechanical Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single face brake</li><li>Power off brake</li><li>Particle brake</li><li>Hysteresis power brake</li><li>Multiple disk brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-mechanical brake market can be categorized into various types based on their function and design. Single face brakes provide stopping power on only one side of the rotor. Power off brakes are designed to engage automatically when power is cut off. Particle brakes use magnetic particles to generate friction. Hysteresis power brakes utilize magnetic hysteresis to control braking force. Multiple disk brakes use multiple disks to increase the braking torque. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
