<p><p>Rear-facing seats are available in three main market types: Rear-Facing-Only seats, Convertible seats, and All-In-One seats. Rear-Facing-Only seats are specifically designed for infants and can only be used in a rear-facing position. Convertible seats can be used both rear-facing for infants and forward-facing for older children. All-In-One seats offer the most versatility as they can be used rear-facing, forward-facing, and as a booster seat, accommodating children from infancy through to their booster seat years.</p></p>
