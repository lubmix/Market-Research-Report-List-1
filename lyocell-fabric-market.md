<p><ul><li>Staple Fiber</li><li>Cross-linked Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lyocell fabric is widely used in the market in two main types: Staple Fiber and Cross-linked Fiber. Staple Fiber Lyocell is produced in short, discrete lengths of fibers that are spun into yarns for fabrics like clothing and household textiles. On the other hand, Cross-linked Fiber Lyocell is chemically modified to enhance its strength and durability, making it suitable for applications like home furnishings, automotive interiors, and medical textiles. Both types of Lyocell offer versatile and sustainable alternatives to traditional textile materials.</p></p>
