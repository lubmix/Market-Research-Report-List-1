<p><p>Anti-reflective glass is a type of glass that is coated with a layer of material designed to reduce glare and reflection. One of the key factors driving the growth of the market is the increasing demand for energy-efficient building materials. Anti-reflective glass helps reduce the amount of heat and glare entering a building, thus reducing the need for artificial lighting and cooling systems. This, in turn, helps in reducing energy consumption and costs.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the growth of the anti-reflective glass market is the rising adoption of solar panels for renewable energy generation. Anti-reflective glass helps in improving the efficiency of solar panels by allowing more sunlight to pass through, thus increasing their energy output.</p><p>In addition, advancements in technology and the development of new coatings and materials are expected to drive the growth of the market further.
