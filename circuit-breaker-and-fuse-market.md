<p><p>Circuit Breaker and Fuse are electrical devices used for the protection of electrical circuits against overload and short circuit conditions. They are designed to interrupt the flow of electricity in case of an overcurrent or a short circuit, thus preventing damage to electrical equipment and potential fires.</p><p>A circuit breaker is an automatic switch that trips and interrupts the flow of current when it detects an abnormal current overload or a short circuit. It can be reset manually after it trips, allowing power to be restored to the circuit. On the other hand, a fuse is a disposable device with a metal strip that melts when subjected to excessive current, interrupting the circuit permanently. A blown fuse needs to be replaced.</p><p>The Circuit Breaker and Fuse Market have been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to the increasing adoption of renewable energy sources, rapid urbanization, and industrialization. The growing demand for electricity and the need for reliable power distribution systems are also driving the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising investments in smart grid infrastructure and the growing construction sector are further boosting the demand for circuit breakers and fuses.
