<p><p>Wafer bonders are divided into two main market types: Semi-Auto Type and Fully Automatic Type. Semi-Auto Type bonders require some level of manual operation, such as loading and unloading wafers, while Fully Automatic Type bonders operate with minimal human intervention. Semi-Auto bonders are more cost-effective for smaller scale operations, while Fully Automatic bonders are preferred for high-volume production due to their efficiency and precision. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs and are essential for various semiconductor and electronic device production processes.</p></p>
