<p><p>Cycle and pedestrian detection systems use different technologies to detect individuals on the road. Radar detection technology uses radio waves to detect the presence of cyclists and pedestrians. Infrared detection technology relies on infrared sensors to detect heat signatures and movement. Video detection technology uses cameras to capture and analyze footage of the road to identify cyclists and pedestrians. Hybrid detection technology combines multiple technologies for more accurate detection.
