<p><p>Dental management software refers to software solutions that assist dental professionals in managing their daily operations. The market for dental management software can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on the dental practice's local servers, providing control and security but requiring maintenance and IT support. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers accessed through the internet. It offers easy accessibility, cost-effectiveness, automatic updates, and data backups, with limited hardware requirements.</p></p>
